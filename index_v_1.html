About De Ning

About Us

CaoYuan Group, established in 2011, is a group company integrating R&D, design, production and sales. It includes five companies. Through 10 years of forging ahead, the output value in 2021 is 850 million yuan, and the annual tax payment is 60 million yuan.

De Ning Energy Saving Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2017. It is a professional guardrail manufacturing and sales enterprise established under Chinese laws. China's intellectual property organization agreement protects the De Ning brand.

De Ning is committed to researching guardrails, barriers and ultra-low energy consumption green building technologies in various industries. The whole manufacturing ecological chain has been built, and the products cover building guardrails, traffic guardrails, unmanned area monitoring guardrails, pasture guardrails, and civil landscaping guardrails. Products in various fields, such as fences, storage inspection guardrails, special safety guardrails for scenic spots, etc.

Factory Overview

The company is established in the operation centre on the 7th floor of Bei You Guohao IOT Ecological Park, with 1,000 square meters.

The company has put into operation two major production bases, both equipped with: large laser cutting machines, large shearing machines, and automatic punching machines, to meet the production requirements of various types of railings.
